SOHO GARDEN

Take yourself up to Nakheel Mall’s rooftop west to the newly opened 8,000 sq. meter Soho Garden in Dubai.
Begin at The Terrace from 5:00 PM to 03:00 AM daily, 7 nights a week on the huge alfresco outdoor space, enjoying sundowners
and taking in views of Dubai and the Palm Jumeirah.
The Terrace offers two bars, one with a central stage for live entertainment,
as well as plenty of comfy sofa seating and multiple shisha options.
From 10:00 PM - 3:00 AM, Thursday to Sunday, continue the night inside the club, offering VIP tables,
local and international entertainment nightly plus an expansive dining and drinks menu.
