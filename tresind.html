TRESIND STUDIO

Since its inception in 2014, Trésind has carved a reputation for being among the region’s leading restaurants. Boasting an exclusive and immersive dining experience, Trésind Studio features an ever-evolving tasting menu by Chef Himanshu, Middle East’s highest-ranking chef.

Operating with a seating capacity of 20 people only, the luxurious dining concept will be housed at the East wing rooftop at Nakheel Mall and will be open from Monday to Sunday, with two dining schedules, 6:00 PM and 12:00 AM.
Trésind Studio approaches the Indian Cuisine with a creative and innovative lens and holds a distinctive style that combines global ingredients with modern cooking techniques and traditional Indian elements.

As part of the Trésind Studio experience, the luxury restaurant will also feature a 5,000 sqft urban outdoor garden that was developed with a local organic farm.

Trèsind Studio was ranked as the fourth best restaurant in the region and received a special accolade for the Art of Hospitality at the ‘50 Best Restaurants 2022 in MENA’ awards.
In addition, Chef Himanshu was also ranked as the 75th on The Best Chef Awards in 2021, ‘Chef of the Year’ at the What's On Dubai Awards 2021 and is the highest ranked chef in the Middle East.
