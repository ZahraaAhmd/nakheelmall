The St. Regis Dubai, The Palm

the hotel

The St. Regis Dubai, The Palm occupies the first 18 floors of The Palm Tower, Nakheel’s 52-storey, 240 metre landmark hotel and residential development on Palm Jumeirah. The hotel consists of 290 rooms and suites with a beautifully designed daylight ballroom, handcrafted restaurant concepts, an indulgent Spa and Exercise Room, as well as two infinity swimming pools overlooking Palm Jumeirah.

Experience the grandeur of Dubai from the 5-star hotel situated on the trunk of the iconic Palm Jumeirah. The luxury hotel is connected to Nakheel Mall with 300 shops, restaurants, entertainment outlets, and convenient access to The View at The Palm, and its own Palm Monorail station. With a size of 43 sqm, all rooms boast a dedicated seating area, floor-to-ceiling windows, and abundant closet and luggage space. Enjoy a tranquil night's rest on a feather-soft mattress with Frette linen and down-filled pillows.

Take advantage of attentive and personalized 24-hour St. Regis Butler Service or savor handcrafted cuisine across four restaurants and lounges. Relax at the hotels two infinity swimming pools, workout in The Exercise Room or unwind at Iridium Spa. Conveniently located to discover Dubai's main attractions, the hotel is one Monorail station away from Aquaventure Waterpark and an easy drive to The Pointe, an iconic waterfront destination, with restaurants, shops.

Sapori D’Italia at Cordelia

Ring in the summer the Italian way with Sapori D’Italia at Cordelia, a delightful and authentic experience for tastemakers and connoisseurs alike. Starting at AED 159 per person, indulge in a four-course menu featuring delectable Italian favorites including an antipasti buffet station with a range of cold cuts and cheeses as well as an array of traditional homemade pasta dishes and delectable pizzas served at your table, from Cavatelli to Pizza Diavola.

Guests can additionally experience a delightful choice of desserts, including a unique, made-to-order tiramisu station with masterfully curated flavors such as classic, matcha, strawberry, and a Dubai favorite, Lotus. To enjoy the full experience, upgrade your package to enjoy a selection of free-flowing grapes for AED 99.

Where: Cordelia, Podium 3 at The St. Regis Dubai, The Palm

When: Every Friday, from 6 pm to 10.30 pm

Validity: Available from July 15

Pricing: AED 159 for a four-course menu and upgrade with AED 99 for free-flowing grape

Jazz Night at The St. Regis Bar

Back by popular demand, the successful Jazz Night will resume at The St. Regis Bar on July 7 and will take place every Thursday, from 7 pm to 11 pm. With legendary jazz tunes in the air, guests will be transported to the Gilded Ages in true Astor style.

For a minimum spend of AED 120, relish the rhythm of cheerful jazz harmonies with the exceptional in-house saxophonist, while exploring a range of exquisite dishes, paired with the signature Glitzy Mary, among other beverages.

Where: St. Regis Bar, Lobby at The St. Regis Dubai, The Palm

When: Every Thursday from 7 to 11 pm

Validity: Ongoing from July 7

Pricing: On consumption with a minimum spend of AED 120

Summer Sanctuary

The St. Regis Dubai, The Palm introduces Summer Sanctuary, an exquisite spa-cation set to elevate guests to the utmost level of relaxation. Available daily from 10 am to 7 pm, explore a distinctive and unparalleled experience beginning with a pampering 60-minute massage at Iridium Spa, followed by a spectacular two-course lunch at the hotel’s flagship venue, Cordelia, and capped off with complimentary access to the hotel’s breathtaking infinity pools. At Iridium Spa, choose any of the 60-minute treatments, from Sound Therapy in Caroline’s Collection to the signature Serenity massage which uses Iridium Hot Stones. For lunch, relish a range of exceptionally dishes at Cordelia with an ever engaging menu.

  • Complimentary pool access at Dip Pool
